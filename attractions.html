Top Attractions

Discover the landmarks that define the spirit of the city.

01

Canton Tower

An iconic 600-meter landmark standing proudly by the Pearl River, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the entire metropolis. Known locally as 'Xiao Man Yao' (slim waist), its stunning architecture and vibrant night illumination make it an absolute must-visit for anyone traveling to Guangzhou.

02

Shamian Island

A tranquil historical area featuring well-preserved European-style architecture from the colonial era, perfect for a leisurely stroll. The tree-lined avenues, bronze statues, and charming boutique cafes transport you to a peaceful bygone era amidst the bustling modern city.

03

Chen Clan Academy

A shining masterpiece of Lingnan architecture, known worldwide for its exquisite wood carvings and traditional Chinese folk arts. Its intricate roof ridges, stone columns, and vast courtyards are an absolute marvel of ancient craftsmanship and cultural heritage.

04

Baiyun Mountain

Known as the "First Spectacular Scene of the City," providing lush greenery, fresh air, and scenic hiking trails for nature lovers. Offering sweeping vistas over the city, it stands out as an idyllic retreat where locals and tourists alike gather to celebrate the beauty of nature.
